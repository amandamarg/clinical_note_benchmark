  - Swelling is nontender.  
  - Remainder of the intraoral examination is within normal limits.  

**Investigations:**  
- **CT Scan:** Complete obliteration of the left maxillary sinus.  
- **Histopathological Examination:**  
  - Fibrous stroma with areas of hyalinization.

  - Tumor cells arranged in solid islands and ductal pattern.  
  - All three histological types present.  
  - No evidence of perineural invasion on serial sectioning.  

**Clinical Differential Diagnosis:**  
- Benign or malignant neoplasm of minor salivary glands.  
- Neoplasm of the maxillary sinus.
