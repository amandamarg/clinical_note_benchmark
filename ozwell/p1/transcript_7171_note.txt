- Postoperative management included warfarin with heparin bridging, early ambulation, and use of elastic stockings

- Severe abdominal pain reported the following day  
- Abdominal x-ray showed two stents located in the abdomen  
- Chest x-ray indicated initial Luminexx stent projecting over the right atrium  
- Intervention: Open-heart surgery performed to retrieve the stent
